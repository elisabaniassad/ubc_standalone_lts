Grade formula (optional — nothing is applied unless you type it): ROUND()+BOOST (boost lifts a grade sitting within N of a UBC boundary), ROUNDUP/ROUNDDOWN, SCALE(n), +BOOST(2). The Grade column always reports whole numbers.
